# Skeleton: Technology Cold Email Template F

## Template
```
Subject: Quick question about {{company}}'s cloud spend

Hi {{first_name}},

Genuine question: is tech debt slowing down feature velocity something your team is actively solving, or has it been put on the back burner?

I ask because we just helped {{company}} go from struggling with the same issue to achieving cut cloud spend by 35% with intelligent optimization — in less than a quarter.

The approach is different from what {{competitor}} offers:
- No lengthy implementation
- Results visible in week one
- Your team keeps their existing workflows

Open to comparing notes on this challenge?

Cheers,
{{sender_name}}
```

## Variables
- `{{company}}`: Company
- `{{competitor}}`: Competitor
- `{{first_name}}`: First Name
- `{{sender_name}}`: Sender Name
