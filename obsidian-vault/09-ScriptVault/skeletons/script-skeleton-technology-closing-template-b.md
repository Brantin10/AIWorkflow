# Skeleton: Technology Closing Template B

## Template
```
Subject: {{first_name}}, ready to make this official?

Hi {{first_name}},

We've covered a lot of ground over the past few weeks, and I want to make sure {{company}} gets the best possible deal before our quarter ends on September 30.

Here's what I can offer if we close by next week:
- 14% discount on annual licensing
- Complimentary onboarding and training (normally $18K)
- Dedicated success manager for the first 6 months
- cut cloud spend by 35% with intelligent optimization — guaranteed in writing

I've attached the final proposal with all the terms we discussed. Your legal team should find everything straightforward.

Can we schedule a {{meeting_length}}-minute call to walk through the agreement together?

Best,
{{sender_name}}
```

- `{{first_name}}`: First Name

- `{{sender_name}}`: Sender Name
