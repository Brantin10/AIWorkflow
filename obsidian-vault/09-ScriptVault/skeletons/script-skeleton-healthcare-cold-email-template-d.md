# Skeleton: Healthcare Cold Email Template D

## Template
```
Subject: Quick question about {{company}}'s compliance score

Hi {{first_name}},

Genuine question: is HIPAA compliance audits consuming entire quarters something your team is actively solving, or has it been put on the back burner?

I ask because we just helped {{company}} go from struggling with the same issue to achieving improve staff satisfaction scores by 30 points — in less than a quarter.

The approach is different from what {{competitor}} offers:
- No lengthy implementation
- Results visible in week one
- Your team keeps their existing workflows

Would a {{meeting_length}}-minute compliance assessment be valuable?

Cheers,
{{sender_name}}
```

## Variables
- `{{company}}`: Company
- `{{competitor}}`: Competitor
- `{{first_name}}`: First Name
- `{{meeting_length}}`: Meeting Length
- `{{sender_name}}`: Sender Name
