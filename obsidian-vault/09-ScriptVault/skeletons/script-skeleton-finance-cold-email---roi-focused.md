# Skeleton: Finance Cold Email - ROI Focused

## Template
```
Subject: {{first_name}}, a question about {{company}}'s {{process}} costs

Hi {{first_name}},

Finance teams at {{company_size}} companies typically spend {{hours_wasted}} hours/month on {{process}} — roughly ${{annual_cost}} in labor.

We automate {{solution_area}} and deliver:
- {{benefit_1}}
- {{benefit_2}}
- {{benefit_3}}

{{reference_company}} saw a {{roi_multiple}}x ROI in {{payback_period}}.

Interested in a {{meeting_length}}-minute ROI walkthrough?

Best,
{{sender_name}}
```

- `{{benefit_3}}`: Benefit 3
- `{{company}}`: Company
- `{{company_size}}`: Company Size
- `{{first_name}}`: First Name
- `{{hours_wasted}}`: Hours Wasted
- `{{meeting_length}}`: Meeting Length
- `{{payback_period}}`: Payback Period
- `{{process}}`: Process
- `{{reference_company}}`: Reference Company
- `{{roi_multiple}}`: Roi Multiple
- `{{sender_name}}`: Sender Name
- `{{solution_area}}`: Solution Area
