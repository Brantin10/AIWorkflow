# Skeleton: SaaS Cold Email Template B

## Template
```
Subject: Quick question about {{company}}'s MRR growth

Hi {{first_name}},

Genuine question: is manual billing processes creating revenue leakage something your team is actively solving, or has it been put on the back burner?

I ask because we just helped {{company}} go from struggling with the same issue to achieving unify your GTM stack into one platform — in less than a quarter.

The approach is different from what {{competitor}} offers:
- No lengthy implementation
- Results visible in week one
- Your team keeps their existing workflows

{{meeting_length}}-min call this week?

Cheers,
Oliver
```

## Variables

- `{{competitor}}`: Competitor
