# Skeleton: Finance Cold Email Template F

## Template

Subject: Quick question about {{company}}'s forecast accuracy

Hi {{first_name}},

Genuine question: is manual reconciliation consuming 40+ hours monthly something your team is actively solving, or has it been put on the back burner?

I ask because we just helped {{company}} go from struggling with the same issue to achieving reduce compliance costs by 40% — in less than a quarter.

The approach is different from what {{competitor}} offers:
- No lengthy implementation

Can I send over a benchmarking report for your industry?

Cheers,
Oliver
