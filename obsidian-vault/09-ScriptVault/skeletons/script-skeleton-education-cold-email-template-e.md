# Skeleton: Education Cold Email Template E

## Template
```
Subject: Quick question about {{company}}'s student retention

Hi {{first_name}},

Genuine question: is student enrollment declining 5% year-over-year something your team is actively solving, or has it been put on the back burner?

I ask because we just helped {{company}} go from struggling with the same issue to achieving boost student engagement by 45% with adaptive content — in less than a quarter.

The approach is different from what {{competitor}} offers:
- No lengthy implementation
- Results visible in week one
- Your team keeps their existing workflows

Should I connect you with a peer institution seeing results?

Cheers,
{{sender_name}}
```

## Variables
- `{{company}}`: Company
- `{{competitor}}`: Competitor

- `{{sender_name}}`: Sender Name
