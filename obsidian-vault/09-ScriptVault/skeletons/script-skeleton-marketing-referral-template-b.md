# Skeleton: Marketing Referral Template B

## Template
```
Subject: {{first_name}}, know anyone dealing with content production bottlenecks slowing campaigns?

Hi {{first_name}},

Since {{company}} has been seeing great results with our platform (26% improvement in organic traffic last quarter), I wanted to ask:

Do you know any peers — maybe other Growth Leads in marketing — who might be dealing with similar challenges?

We'd love to help them the way we've helped {{company}}. And of course, we have a referral program:
- ${{referral_bonus}} for each qualified introduction
- Their team gets an extended trial period
- You get early access to our beta program

No pressure at all — but if anyone comes to mind, a quick intro email is all it takes.

Thanks for being a great partner,
{{sender_name}}
```

- `{{first_name}}`: First Name

- `{{sender_name}}`: Sender Name
