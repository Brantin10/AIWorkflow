# Skeleton: Insurance Cold Email Template E

## Template

```
Subject: Quick question about {{company}}'s customer NPS

Hi {{first_name}},

Genuine question: is customer onboarding requiring 7+ touchpoints something your team is actively solving, or has it been put on the back burner?

I ask because we just helped {{company}} go from struggling with the same issue to achieving reduce claims processing time to 3 days — in less than a quarter.

The approach is different from what {{competitor}} offers:
- No lengthy implementation
- Results visible in week one
- Your team keeps their existing workflows

Open to seeing a demo of our underwriting platform?

Cheers,
Oliver
```
