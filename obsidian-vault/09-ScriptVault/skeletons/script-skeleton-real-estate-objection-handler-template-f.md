# Skeleton: Real Estate Objection Handler Template F

## Template
```
Completely understand, {{first_name}}. Timing is everything.

Can I ask — is property valuation models lagging market conditions getting better on its own, or is it something that compounds over time? In our experience, real estate teams that wait another quarter typically see:
- deal flow volume decline by an additional 20%
- Costs increase by $28K+ per quarter
- Competitors who act sooner gain a 9-month advantage

I don't want to create false urgency — but I do want to make sure {{company}} has the data to make an informed decision on timing. Can I send over a quick impact analysis?
```
